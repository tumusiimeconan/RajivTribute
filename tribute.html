In Loving Memory of Rajiv Ruparelia

1990 – 2025

Rajiv Ruparelia was a visionary Ugandan entrepreneur, philanthropist, and leader whose contributions left an indelible mark on Uganda. As a key figure in the Ruparelia Group, he drove transformative growth in real estate, developing landmark properties that elevated Uganda’s urban landscape. His commitment to education fostered opportunities for countless students through institutions like Victoria University. Through his philanthropy, Rajiv supported community initiatives, improving lives and inspiring hope across the nation. His legacy of compassion, innovation, and leadership will continue to inspire generations.
